## Hardware Assembly & Wiring

These photos show the detailed process of assembling the RV Link hardware.

<CardGrid>
    <Card title="Start: Board Prep" icon="setting">

    </Card>
    <Card title="Thermal Management">

        Apply heat transfer pads to the Raspberry Pi.
    </Card>
    <Card title="NVMe Adapter">
        ![top down view of the pi with the Waveshare PCIe To M.2 Adapter Board (E) installed and the standoffs installed in all 4 corners, no nvme installed](/img/assembly/assembly_20.jpg)

    </Card>
    <Card title="NVMe Installation">
        ![top down view of the pi with the Waveshare PCIe To M.2 Adapter Board (E) installed and the standoffs installed in all 4 corners with nvme installed](/img/assembly/assembly_21.jpg)
        Secure the NVMe drive to the adapter board.
    </Card>
    <Card title="CAN HAT Installation">
        ![top down view of the "Waveshare CAN HAT +" installed on the top of the pi using the 4 standoffs](/img/assembly/assembly_22.jpg)

    </Card>
    <Card title="Side View">
